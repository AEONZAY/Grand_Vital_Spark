PREVENTION IS BETTER THAN CURE!

Prevention truly is better than cure. While cures exist for many ailments, preventing them in the first place is often easier, cheaper, and less stressful. Imagine avoiding a nasty illness altogether compared to dealing with doctor visits, medications, and potential recovery time.
Prevention, through healthy habits or safety measures, allows you to live a more vibrant life and reduces the burden on healthcare systems.
So, take steps to prevent what you can.
